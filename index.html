DIY-signer is an interior decoration app to help users design their rooms layout by visualizing new furniture and decor in their current space through augmented reality as well as purchasing the displayed products through an online marketplace. While we may no longer be in full lockdown, many of us are still working remotely and generally still spending a lot more time at home than we ever have historically. Being able to personalize, and update your space has always been important, but when you are spending the great majority of your time at home it becomes paramount.

My Role

My major contribution was prototyping, designing, and wireframing the workspace and furnishing screens, however I was also involved in user interviews, UX research, competetor analsysis, user flow and user testing and analysis along side my teammates.
